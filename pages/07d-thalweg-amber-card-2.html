Drone Tracker

Crowdsourced Airspace Observation Network
Concept

Citizens report drone sightings using smartphone camera and sensor data. Multiple independent observations from different locations are combined to triangulate positions and reconstruct flight paths across monitored airspace.

How It Works
01
Field Reporting
Observers capture sightings through a mobile app that records video, GPS coordinates, compass bearing, and device orientation. Reports are timestamped and queued for submission.
02
Triangulation
Overlapping reports from multiple observers are correlated by time and bearing to compute 3D positions. Confidence increases with the number of independent sightings for each event.
03
Pattern Mapping
Reconstructed flight paths are aggregated over time to reveal recurring routes, activity hotspots, and temporal patterns across the observation network.

Audioreactive

Real-Time Audio-Reactive Visual Synthesis
Concept

Live microphone input feeds into musical feature extraction, driving continuous navigation through a generative latent space. Bass frequencies control large-scale structure, mids drive composition and movement, highs modulate fine detail and texture.

How It Works
01
Audio Capture
Live audio is captured from the microphone and decomposed into spectral features — chromagram, onset detection, and frequency band energy — updated each frame.
02
Feature Mapping
Extracted features are mapped to latent space coordinates using learned projections. Smooth interpolation between frames ensures visual continuity while preserving musical responsiveness.
03
Generative Synthesis
The generator network produces continuous visuals from the current latent position. Post-processing applies color grading and temporal blending to create fluid motion.
