The threat

The sky over Europe is contested.

Since 2022, unidentified drones have closed airports, shadowed warships, and circled nuclear sites across Europe. The wave broke in autumn 2025, and it clusters where it spills out of the war in Ukraine — the Nordics, the Baltics, Poland.

What it is

Two ways to put a drone on the map.

01 · Static

See a drone and photograph it. One report carries your position, the bearing, and the time.

02 · Live

Hovering overhead? Hold your phone up and track it live — the app follows where you point, in real time.
