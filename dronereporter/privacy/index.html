Privacy Policy

Drone Reporter — Effective February 17, 2026

Kinami ("we", "us", "our") operates the DroneReporter mobile application (the "App"). This policy describes what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

Account information

When you sign in with Apple, we receive your email address and name (if you choose to share it). We use this solely to create and manage your account.

Drone observations

When you submit an observation, we collect:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude) of your location at the time of the report
  • Photo taken with your device camera
  • Device sensor data: compass bearing, pitch, roll, and estimated field of view
  • Device make and model (e.g., "Apple iPhone 14 Pro")
  • Timestamp of the observation
  • Any optional text you provide (description, drone type, behaviour)

Technical data

  • Authentication tokens stored on your device to keep you signed in
  • IP address logged by our servers and third-party services as part of normal HTTPS connections

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • We do not collect contacts, calendar, health, or financial data
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the service: Display drone observations on the map, manage your account, and let you review or delete your reports.
  • Improve the service: Aggregate, anonymised observation data may be used to identify drone activity patterns.
  • Communicate with you: We may email you about important changes to your account or this policy. We do not send marketing emails.

3. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data they receive
Apple Sign-In Authentication Your Apple ID credential
CARTO Map tiles Your IP address and the map area you are viewing
Google Cloud Platform Backend hosting (europe-west4, Netherlands) All data listed above, stored and processed in the EU

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All backend data is stored on Google Cloud Platform in europe-west4 (Netherlands).
  • API communication uses HTTPS with TLS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens on your device are stored using the platform keychain (iOS Keychain).
  • Photos are stored in a private Google Cloud Storage bucket accessible only to our backend service.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Observations are retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Server logs (IP addresses, request metadata) are retained for up to 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • View your observations in the "My Observations" screen.
  • Delete individual observations by swiping to delete.
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at privacy@kinami.dk.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at the same address.

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, port, and restrict processing of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kinami.dk.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
